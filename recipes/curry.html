Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1-2lbs of chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 16 oz of coconut milk
  • 8 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 16 oz of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Heat up the olive oil on a pan to medium heat and add the diced onions till it's slightly brown.
  • Add the garlic and ginger for 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add chicken and cook until cooked.
  • Add coconut milk, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Stir to combine.
  • Add curry powder and stir for 1 minute.
  • Simmer for 20-30 minutes until the sauce thickens. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  • Optional but encouraged: Always taste the sauce so you can add seasonings to your liking. I always like extra seasonings like paprika, cayenne pepper, and more curry powder for extra flavor.
  • Serve over rice and enjoy!
