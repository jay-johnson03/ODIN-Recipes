Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1-2lbs of chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, minced
  • 4 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 16 oz of coconut milk
  • 8 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 16 oz of tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • Heat up the olive oil on a pan to medium heat and add the diced onions till it's slightly brown.
  • Add the garlic and ginger for 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add chicken and cook until cooked.
